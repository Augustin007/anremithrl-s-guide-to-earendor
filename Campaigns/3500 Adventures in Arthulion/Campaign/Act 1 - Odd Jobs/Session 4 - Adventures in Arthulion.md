They work a forge for the "bandits", who turn out to be mostly be outcasts, exiles, and outlaws of the Sonorous empires, and thus take a liking for Duthrim.

Jason thinks about forming them into some form of rebellion, instead of measly bandits. He and Duthrim befriend the bandits.

Jason challenges the bandit leader to a duel and bests him, forcing him to yield, and taking command of the bandits.
