They reach a town and buy horses and begin riding to the Emerald Shores as fast as they can. It takes them three days of riding and most of their money. 

They arrive at the gates and camp just outside, but decide they cannot give him time to consolidate his power, so they show up and demand an audience. 

Theriad tries to kill Mabry but fails and is arrested and scheduled for execution. Duthrim attempts to run negotiations of sorts, but Mabry finds the idea laughable, and doesn't let the two leave the palace. But by then Jason had already escaped.
