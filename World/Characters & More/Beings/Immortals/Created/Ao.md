Ao, the King of Order, was created by The First Cause before the formation of Time and Space. He is equivalent to the realm of [[Mechanus]]. He is the force of frigidity.

He is the sole source of the Ordered Quiddity.

He is bonded to [[Isfet]], and loves her.
