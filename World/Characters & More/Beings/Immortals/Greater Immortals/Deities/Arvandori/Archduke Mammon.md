The Archduke Mammon is a lesser god who rose to notable rank during the Sundered War. He is a descendant of the Elohim. He married [[Tiamat]] and with her rules [[Minauros]], one of the nine hells. 

He generally chooses to show himself with a long beard and a wide smile on his face, sitting on a throne covered in gold coins. 

During the war, he bribed leaders among men with promises of great riches if they would follow him.
