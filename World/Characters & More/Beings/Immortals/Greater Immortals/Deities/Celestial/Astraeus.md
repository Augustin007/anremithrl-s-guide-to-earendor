Astraeus is a Celestial who remained a member of the original [[Celestial Council]] and did not participate in the [[The Sundering War]]. Before the Sundering War he was known as the God of Starlight. He is still worshiped in some areas of Hatreth. He was one of the three gods whose quiddity was Arcane.
