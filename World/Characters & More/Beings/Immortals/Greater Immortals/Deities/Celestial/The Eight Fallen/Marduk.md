Marduk was a close friend of [[Asmodeus]], one of the leading Celestials. He was known for his valor and courage, often standing beside Asmodeus in the heat of battle. He aided in the destruction of the Dusk.[^1]

Marduk was one of the eight who fell against the [[Epic Dragons]], his bravery immortalized in the annals of Celestial history.

[^1]: *Source:* [[Echoes of Divinity]]
