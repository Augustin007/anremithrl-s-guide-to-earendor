
Tiamat was born of [[Rhea]] and one of the [[Epic Dragons]]when she was taken by the [[Epic Dragons]]. Her cousin, [[Bahamut]], was born similarly of [[Iris]]. 

She earned the emnity of [[Auril]] by virtue of her existence. 

She spent much time converting mortals to her will and giving them the power of dragons.

She married [[Archduke Mammon]] and rules [[Minauros]] at his side. 

She reconciled with her half sister Auril when [[Tartarus]] took over the nine hells.
