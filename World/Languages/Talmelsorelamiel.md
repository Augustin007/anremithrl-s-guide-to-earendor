## 1. Introduction
The language's endonym is talmelsorelamiel, but most mortal tongues name it Ancient, Celestial, or Arvandoric. Certain varieties were to name it Hellish or Abyssal, as it is the same tongue those beings speak, though there are differences within such dialects. 

This language is the language of the beginning of time. A language of thoughts and concepts. While it has written and audible aspects, its designs were far more complex than that.

## 3. Morphology

### 3.1 Word Structure

Arvandoric words = morpheme bundles. Template (order irrelevant):

```
[Root] ± [Ontology] ± [Role] ± [Aspect] ± [Intent] ± [Tone] ± [Essense]
```

All elements commute and associate.

### 3.2 Ontology

-um (mortal), -el (divine), -uth (primordial).

### 3.3 Roles (Cases)

Nominative -a, Accusative -in, Genitive -es, Dative -or, Instrumental -ik, Locative -ur, Vocative -oʀ, Causal -ath, Metaphysical -æn, Entropic -xun.

### 3.4 Aspect

-el (eternal), -ri (iterative), -ta (momentary), -en (becoming), -ul (decaying), -is (suspended), -or (recursive), -ɬi (paradoxical).

### 3.5 Intents (Moods)

-a (declarative), -eʔ (directive), -on (evocative), -ux (banishing), -kar (binding), -soʀ (summoning), -al (recalling), -the (veiling).

### 3.6 Tone (Respect)

ʀ- (respectful), ø- (neutral), x- (derisive).

### 3.7 Essense

-θa (physical), -na (spiritual), -re (historical), -mi (cosmic).

---

## 4. Syntax

- **Word order**: None. Morphemes encode all relations.
    
- **Word-as-sentence**: A single word can contain subject, object, mood, and Essense.
    
- **Clause chaining**: Multiple words form paradoxes, contrasts, emphasis.
    
- **Negation**: encoded via shadowed vowels and entropic markers.
    

---

## 5. Semantics & Pragmatics

- Respect vs. derision encoded morphologically.

- Ambiguity is structural: paradoxical morphemes (-ɬi) allow contradictory truths.

- Uttering a divine name instantiates its essence.

- Registers: respectful, neutral, derisive — obligatory in all forms.

## 6. Lexicon

### Core Roots

- tal- = essence, name, identity

- kar- = bind, decree, establish

- neθa- = soul, animating force

- kor- = death, cessation

- dra- = darkness, concealment

- sol- = light, revelation

- mel- = weave, join, connect

- soʀ- = call forth, summon

- zar- = destruction, breaking

- lem- = knowledge, perception

- vaɾ- = power, force
