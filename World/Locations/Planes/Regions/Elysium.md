# Elysium

## Introduction

Elysium, the transcendent realm of peace and sanctuary, exists beyond the known boundaries of the [[Eärendor|Universe]]. Governed by the primordial entity [[Selune]], this plane stands in stark contrast to the discord and strife prevalent in other cosmic regions. It is a haven for those seeking respite from the Eternal Wars and the tumultuous conflicts that have shaped the Dawn, Dusk, and their offspring.

## Nature of Elysium

### A Realm of Tranquility
Elysium is characterized by its serene landscapes and harmonious atmosphere. It is a place where the turmoil of the universe seems distant, and peace pervades every corner. The landscapes of Elysium vary, featuring lush gardens, tranquil lakes, and gentle meadows, all existing in a state of perfect natural balance.

### Sanctuary of Souls
Elysium serves as a sanctuary for weary souls and beings who have grown tired of endless conflict. Here, entities from all corners of the Universe can find solace, healing from the scars of cosmic battles, and respite from their eternal struggles.

## Inhabitants

### Selune's Presence
Selune, the embodiment of light and sanctuary, presides over Elysium. Her benevolent influence is felt throughout the plane, ensuring peace and harmony for all who dwell within.

### Refugees of War
Elysium is home to a diverse array of beings who have sought refuge from the Eternal Wars and other conflicts. Among them are notable entities like [[Telos]] and [[Nehusta]], who found solace in Selune’s realm after fleeing the Universe.

### Guardians of Peace
Elysium is protected by guardians who ensure that the sanctity of the realm is maintained. These beings are devoted to Selune and her ideals of peace and harmony, vigilantly upholding the cosmic truce that governs the plane.

## Role in the Cosmic Tapestry

### A Neutral Ground
Elysium serves as neutral ground in the cosmic landscape. Its very existence provides a counterbalance to the chaos and destruction that pervade other planes, offering a glimpse of what a universe at peace could look like.

### Diplomatic Haven
Elysium is often used as a meeting place for cosmic entities seeking to negotiate or discuss matters without the threat of violence. The plane's neutral status makes it an ideal ground for diplomacy and peaceful resolution of conflicts.

## Mystical Properties

### Healing and Restoration
The plane is imbued with properties that promote healing, both physical and spiritual. Those who spend time in Elysium often find their wounds, whether inflicted by sword or sorrow, mending more swiftly than elsewhere.

### Sanctuary of Knowledge and Wisdom
Elysium is also a repository of ancient knowledge and wisdom, preserved away from the ravages of war and decay. Scholars and seekers of truth often journey here in search of enlightenment.

## Conclusion

Elysium stands as a testament to the possibility of peace and harmony in a universe otherwise consumed by conflict. Governed by Selune, it remains a bastion of tranquility and sanctuary, offering solace to all who seek refuge from the chaos of the Eternal Wars. In the grand scheme of the Universe, Elysium is a beacon of hope, a reminder of the potential for peace amidst turmoil.
