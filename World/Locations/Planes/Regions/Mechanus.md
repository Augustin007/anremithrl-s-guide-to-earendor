# Mechanus

## Introduction

Mechanus, the realm of the King of Order, [[The Queen of Chaos and The King of Order|Ao]], is a plane of existence that epitomizes the very essence of order, precision, and law. As the cosmic domain of one of the primordial entities, it stands as a testament to the structured aspect of the Universe, reflecting the meticulous and unyielding nature of its ruler. This document delves into the lore of Mechanus, exploring its characteristics, inhabitants, and the role it plays in the grand scheme of [[Eärendor|Universe]].

## Nature of Mechanus

### Realm of Absolute Order
Mechanus is a plane where every action, every moment, and every aspect adheres to a precise and immutable order. It is a realm of perfect geometry and unvarying patterns, where the chaos and unpredictability of the rest of the Universe hold no sway.

### The Clockwork Universe
Often visualized as an immense, interlocking mechanism, Mechanus is akin to a vast clockwork. Its landscapes are composed of gears, cogs, and wheels, all functioning in perfect harmony. The plane’s very fabric operates like a grand, cosmic machine, each part essential and every movement calculated.

## Inhabitants

### The Modrons
Mechanus is inhabited by the Modrons, a race of beings who are the embodiment of order and structure. Each Modron has a specific function and place within the hierarchy of Mechanus, operating with unwavering precision and dedication to maintaining the order of the plane.

### The King of Order: Ao
Ao, the King of Order, presides over Mechanus with a dispassionate and calculating gaze. He is the epitome of order, his will manifesting as the unbreakable laws that govern the plane. Ao rarely interacts directly with other realms, preferring to observe and maintain the balance of his domain.

## Role in the Cosmic Order

### Bastion of Predictability
In a Universe where chaos and unpredictability are constant forces, Mechanus stands as a bastion of predictability and stability. It serves as a counterbalance to the chaotic realms, ensuring that order remains a fundamental aspect of the cosmic balance.

### Sanctuary of Law
Mechanus is often sought by beings who crave certainty and structure, or by those who wish to study the nature of cosmic order. Its unchanging nature makes it an ideal place for contemplation and understanding of the laws that underpin reality.

## Mystical Properties

### The Source of Cosmic Law
Mechanus is the wellspring of cosmic law and order. The principles that emanate from this plane influence the fundamental laws that govern the Universe, impacting everything from the orbits of celestial bodies to the moral codes of civilizations.

### The Great Convergence
At the heart of Mechanus lies the Great Convergence, a nexus where the plane’s energies are most potent. It is here that the essence of order is at its strongest, and where Ao’s presence can be most keenly felt.

## Conclusion

Mechanus, as the realm of Ao, the King of Order, is an essential component of the cosmic equilibrium. Its existence ensures that order remains a constant force in the Universe, counterbalancing the ever-present chaos. In the grand tapestry of existence, Mechanus is a symbol of stability, a reminder that even in a Universe teeming with unpredictability, order persists as an unyielding principle.
