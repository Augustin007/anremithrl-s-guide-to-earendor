There are six True Planes:
[[Ethereal Plane]]
There are four sub-planes of the Ethereal Plane:
   [[Phlogiston]], the old home of Asgorath, and the sub-plane of Aether.
   [[Chronosium]], the old home of Arambar, and the sub-plane of Time
   [[Astral Plane]], the sub-plane of the dead, where the mortal souls go to when they are out of time.
 
[[Material Plane]]
The Material Plane is divided into three different sub-planes.
  [[Shadowfell]] (The sub-plane of Memory)
  [[Ephemeral Lands]] (The sub-plane of Morality)
  [[Feywild]]  (The sub-plane of Illusion)

Elemental Planes
  [[Caverns of Stillness]] (The Plane of Earth)
  [[Undying Pyre]] (The Plane of Fire)
  [[Cresting Spires]] (The Plane of Water)
  [[Gentle Zephyr]] (The Plane of Air)


Paraelemental Planes
These are the borders between the Elemental Planes
  [[Frostfell]] (The Plane of Ice (Between water and air))
  [[Swamp of Oblivion]] (The Plane of Ooze (Between water and earth))
  [[Great Conflagration]] (The Plane of Smoke (Between air and fire))
  [[Fountains of Creation]] (The Plane of Magma (Between earth and fire))
  [[Boiling Seas]] (The Plane of Vapor (between water and fire)) 
  [[Floating Mountains]] (The Plane of Sand (Between air and earth))
