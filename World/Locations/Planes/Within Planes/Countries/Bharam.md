The Cult of Bharam
--------------------
The cult of Bharam is led by [[Bechel of Bharam]], a soothsayer who has existed for centuries under the guise of protecting mortals. 

The Dreamers of Bharam's Alliances
-------------------------------------
The cult of Bharam has allied itself with Tartarus and the Betrayer Gods, making them even more powerful and dangerous than they already were. They are not afraid to use their strength to enforce their will on others.
