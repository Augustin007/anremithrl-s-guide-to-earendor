|[[Session 36 - Adventures in Arthulion ]]|[[Adventures in Arthulion]]|[[Session 38 - Adventures in Arthulion]]|

Duthrim and Lorim are eating dinner with Nabrin. Someone knocks at their door. They open it to see a blue-skinned silver-handed tiefling woman in a hood. Lorim and Duthrim quickly guess she is a threat and Duthrim blocks the doorway and introduces himself. The woman says she's a friend of Janvar, and pulls out the old Whist forged for his helmet, saying Janvar gave it to her just in case she needed to offer it as proof. Duthrim keeps the door guarded, but Lorim insists Janvar is in danger, and Duthrim and Lorim reluctantly allow her to talk a bit more.

Still outside, she tells them that he was chasing a method to resurrect his sister and Miranjen, which rings a little too true to Duthrim and Lorim, and that Janvar had gotten himself into trouble. Lorim and Duthrim discuss the fact that she is referring to Janvar, not Jason, and that they don't really have a duty to go around rescuing Jason, but are convinced Lilith is telling the truth. They decide to hear her out a bit and at least offer her something to eat and somewhere to stay the night, but explain they aren't willing to do more. Neither of them notice Lilith pocket their sending stone and replace it with a fake.

Theriad tries to contact the Attendents of Knowledge only to find that he is disbelieved and had been usurped in the meantime. New members of the council he has hardly met think he is nothing but an impersonator. The teleportation circles have fallen into ruin. Communication back and forth had long stopped. Theriad cannot communicate what was going on.

Theriad seems shell-shocked after returning to the mortal plane, though he insists he is fine. Renk spends a lot of time with him. He is much quieter than he used to be, and his skin and hair are now pale white instead of the older dark brown, though Theriad tries to dye his hair and get his skin to regain colour. He is constantly tense and disassociated, and doesn't really talk about the years since Renk left him. She keeps trying to get to him and he keeps trying to insist that he is fine. Jolvin gently reminds his wife that it's only been a day, and tries to message Duthrim and Lorim, who do not respond. They assume it is too late.

That same night, Janvar arrives at their place followed by a woman who introduces herself as Bechel. Janvar tells Theriad about the Shard of the Forgotten One, and that Bechel is helping him find it. He tells Theriad that Lilith has the Shard, and is working with the betrayer gods, and asks for Theriad's help. Renk offers to go in Theriad's stead, but he and Jolvin talk her down, since she and Jolvin have still a family. Jolvin is especially against leaving their child this young, because of his own history. 

Once Lilith retreats to the guest room, Duthrim and Lorim have a hushed conversation. Duthrim does not particularly want to deal with this, and Lorim is in agreement. The fake sending stone goes off, containing a message (falsified by Lilith), of Jason begging for their help. Duthrim and Lorim identify that the message is fake, but the references to their grandkids make them worry there *is* a credible danger to them as well, and they discuss it further. Lilith, meanwhile, gets the message from Jolvin meant for Duthrim and Lorim appraising her of Theriad's return, and hours later another informing her of Janvar's arrival over there.

Duthrim and Lorim try to message their kids, to no avail, finding that the sending stone is not working. Lorim is suspicious of this. Duthrim and Lorim agree to head to the Citadel to see Birkgar and Irna Ilern, and then to Crof to see Jolvin and Renk, leaving Nabrim with Halvor and Irna their neighbours, as Nabrim is too old to be left alone and to travel. When they talk to Lilith in the morning, explaining the fake message, Lilith agrees to help them on their journey to the Citadel.

They begin travelling.

The next morning, in Crof, and Jolvin and Renk's household, everyone begins to worry about Duthrim and Lorim, who haven't responded, and Janvar guesses that Lilith got to them. Theriad agrees to help Janvar and Bechel, but will spend a week enchanting a new sending stone so he can talk to his daughter, Renk, regularly, and so that he can recover.
