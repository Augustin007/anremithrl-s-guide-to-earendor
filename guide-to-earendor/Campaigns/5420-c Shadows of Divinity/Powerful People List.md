List of people put together by William Treaty to potentially approach. 


1. [[Ashdilkir]] - "Me". Used to have the labyrinth stone.
2. [[Singollo]] - Elven king, Nephew of Araushnee killed Draco-Glaumhot during the Sundered War with Glorfindel. Married to Elian. 
3. [[Glorfindel]] - Elven Warrior and General, see above.
4. [[Elian]] - An Elessar of considerable power, queen of the elves, holds one of the Silanderi.
5. [[Lady Elmenore]] the Unforgiving - An Eladrin and Singollo's cousin. Queen of the Eladrin in the Feywild.
6. [[Aeoife]] and [[Scathatch]] - Twin sisters, who were the children of the first vampires. They inherited some vampiric traits, and have devoted their immortal lives to studying combat.
7.  [[Xandred Valerius]] - Discovered Lichdom, powerful necromancer. Dean of necromancy. The only Lich who does not _seem_ to need constant feeding on recently dead souls to maintain his immortality.
8. [[Eleutheros]] - The Traveler, a powerful elven sorcerer who is ascending to godhood and created the feywild and sometimes called the first Eladrin. Lady Elmenore's brother.
9. [[Elira]] the wrath - Tried to mimic the Raven Queen's ascension and caused the Gnome-killing pestilance.
10. [[Hechilspet]] - A warlord and tyrant who ascended to godhood until he was captured in a demiplane prison by the Traveler. Recently went missing.
11. [[Seraphelle]] - An artist and engineer. She was granted immortality by Nehusta. She is considered the goddess of art.
12. [[Thorne]] - The soul collector. A paladin of justice. Once borrowed the Labyrinth-stone from Ashdilkir to make his own. Lawful to the point of obsession and insanity. He devoted his life to capturing the souls people who broke their oaths. He devoted himself so fully that even in death could not stop him. 
13. [[Lysandra]] was a priestess of Selune, the primordial of light. In a moment of desperation to save her people from a terrible plague, she offered her life to Selune. Touched by her devotion, Selune transformed her into a celestial being.
14. [[Valus]] The Eternal Warrior - A sellsword very highly paid. He claims he managed to purchase immortality. No one knows from who or how.
15. [[Varook]] converted himself into a construct of stone and iron, mimicking Telos. He guards the city where he lived from all dangers. He once killed a great dragon which came to the small town. He never has been known to leave.
16. [[Ghalen]] was a scholar obsessed with the mysteries of time. After decades of research, he discovered a way to step outside the normal flow of time, effectively becoming ageless. Ghalen performed the ritual and vanished from the material plane. He now exists in a pocket dimension, where he continues his studies, occasionally stepping back into the world. He is the only chronomancer.
17. [[Theriad Keladri]] - Cleric of the god of knowledge. Lives in lorekeep. 
18. [[Lucy Iwaelfhem]]  - A fae who fought and drove away the dragon Tormac along with Baeor, [[Richard Iwaelfhem]], and [[Daedroy Belkar]].
19. [[Baeor Dechaius]] - A barbarian-fighter half-dwarf half goliath who fought Tormac.
20. [[Cordast Tresant]] - Tricked Orthax and stoll enough of his power to attain a quasi-Deity status.
21. [[Tal'berius Tarrilion]] - Obviously.
