Zalkeshar makes a deal with Unther in order to get revenge after his wife is killed in a robbery orchestrated by Neithen Thalion. He calls an old debt owed to him from his friend Norvias.

Norvias buys gear. Studies up on Neithen. To get information on Neithen, signed magical contract to do one job without pay for the Hidden Thieves.

Norvias bribes a barkeep for more concentrated alchohol than legally allowed. He decides to steal the bribe back and followed him to his family. Failed to seak in. When spotted, accidentally kills the father in front of his children to steal the money back. Undeterred, he threatened the two children so that the mother gave away her valueables.

---

Far away, Neithen is on the run, attempting to get back to his lordship in Rymfel. He pretends to be an insane old man and requisitions the help of a woman he meets named Ophiriel. He lies several times. Deliberately injures himself to seem harmless and doddering. Travels with her to the border. Lies to the guards. Climbs up a tree. Ophiriel convinces them he is mentally insane, which is super easy because she thinks he _is_. Ophiriel confiscates his weapons when she discovers them.
