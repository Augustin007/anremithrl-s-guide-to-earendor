Zal'keshar is arrested for the murder committed by Norvias and interrogated. A cloaked figure, a vigilante identifying himself as Ravenhead, talks to Theriad Keladri about several strange recent events before beginning an investigation on the murder.
Norvias tracks Faust's scent to Morgana's home and meets Morgana's parents, who attack him. They are revealed to be hemalurgically spiked and part of the Kollus. Learning more about the Den, Norvias meets Mials and agrees to potentially help them and join the Den, staying the night with them.
A bounty is placed on Lancel Moonwatcher.
