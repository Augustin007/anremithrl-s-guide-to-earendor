### Adhesion(Order)
Surge ability: Bonding Touch— Once per day, you can choose two surfaces you can see that are touching within 15 feet and bind them together with a magical bond. This bond is almost unbreakable by physical force (DC (20,30,40) Strength check to break). The bond lasts for 1 hour unless dispelled or destroyed by magic.
Spells:
- Lesser sphere spells
	- [[spell:mending]], [[spell:hold-person]], [[spell:warding-wind]]
- Greater sphere spells
	- [[spell:hold-monster]], [[spell:magic-mouth]]
- Grand sphere spells
	- [[spell:glyph-of-warding]], [[spell:immovable-object]]

### Division(chaos magic)
Surge ability: Sunder—You can touch an object or creature and cause a part of it to break away. This ability deals 5d10 force (7d10 greater, 10d10 grand) damage to objects or forces a creature to make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw or suffer from Sundered—losing 1d10 hit points and suffering a penalty to their next attack roll.

Spells: 
- Lesser sphere spells
	- [[spell:chaos-bolt]],[[spell:shatter]]
- Greater sphere spells
	- [[spell:disintegrate]], [[spell:dispel-magic]]

	- [[annihilation]], [[spell:prismatic-spray]]

### Progression(Positive & Divine)
Surge ability: Rapid Healing—Choose a creature within 30 feet. They regain hit points equal to 5d8 + your Wisdom modifier. If this heals them above their maximum, the excess is converted into temporary hit points.
Spells: 
- Lesser sphere spells
	- [[spell:cure-wounds]], [[spell:bless]], [[spell:healing-word]], [[spell:goodberry]]
- Greater sphere spells
	- [[spell:mass-healing-word]], [[spell:lesser-restoration]]
- Grand sphere spells
	- [[spell:greater-restoration]], [[spell:power-word-heal]], [[spell:mass-cure-wounds]]

### Transformation(Chaos and Order magic)
Surge ability: Reshape Matter—You can reshape a non-magical object no larger than 5 feet in any dimension. This transformation is permanent unless dispelled by powerful magic. For one minute, you can also transform willing creatures as per Polymorph.
Spells:
- Lesser sphere spells
	- [[spell:alter-self]], [[Meta/Homebrew/RAW/Character/Extra_Abilities/Spells/spell:Polymorph|spell:Polymorph]]
- Greater sphere spells
	- [[spell:shapechange]]
- Grand sphere spells
	- [[spell:true-polymorph]]

### Transportation(Arcane or Divine magic)
Surge ability: Warp Step—You can teleport yourself and up to eight willing creatures up to 60 feet (anywhere you can see, anywhere you can remember) to an unoccupied space. This ability can be used a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier before needing to recharge.
Sphere spells:
- Lesser sphere spells
	- [[spell:misty-step]], [[spell:dimension-door]]
- Greater sphere spells
	- [[spell:teleport]], [[spell:plane-shift]]
- Grand sphere dpells
	- [[Meta/Homebrew/RAW/Character/Extra_Abilities/Spells/spell:Gate|spell:Gate]], [[spell:plane-shift]]

### Cohesion(Negative magic)
Surge ability: Structural Collapse—You can touch a wall or similar structure and weaken its cohesion. This causes a DC 15, 20, or 25 saving throw, on a fail, after one round, the structure collapses, dealing 4d10 damage to creatures within a 10-foot radius.
Spells
- Lesser sphere spells
	- [[spell:ray-of-enfeeblement]], [[spell:inflict-wounds]], [[spell:shadow-blade]], [[spell:passwall]]
- Greater sphere spells
	- [[spell:finger-of-death]], [[spell:animate-dead]]
- Grand sphere spells
	- [[spell:disintegrate]], [[spell:power-word-kill]]

### Illumination(Chaos and arcane magic, Fey magic)
Surge ability: Blinding Light—You create a 30-foot sphere of blinding light centered on you. All creatures within the area must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or be blinded for 1 minute.
Spells:
