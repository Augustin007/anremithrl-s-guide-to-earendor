Hekate is one of the three celestials with arcane quiddity. 

She gave mortals the ability to use magic.

While she initially stood with [[Asmodeus]] and aided in the construction of [[The Nine Hells]], she betrayed him and eventually joined the [[Righteous Council]]. 

She is the patron of [[Morgana]].[^1]

She was killed by [[Tartarus]] during [[The Shattering]].

[^1]: *Source:* [[Shadows of Divinity]]
