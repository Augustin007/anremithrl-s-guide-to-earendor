Gond and Nebelum were born of identical Edicts. On freeing, they loved creation, and studied far and wide, exploring every location and plane. They accumulated much knowledge and where known for their intelligence.[^1]

Gond was the twin brother of [[Nebelum]]. Both were known for venturing far and wide, collecting much knowledge. He fell during the battle against the [[Epic Dragons]].
