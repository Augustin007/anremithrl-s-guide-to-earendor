Lux was a friend of [[Gond]] and [[Nebelum]].[^1]

Lux was the only Celestial who had sided with [[Vhel]] in advocating for retreat during the battle against the [[Epic Dragons]]. He was known for his brilliance and clarity of thought. He fell during the battle.

[^1]: *Source:* [[Adventures in Arthulion]]
