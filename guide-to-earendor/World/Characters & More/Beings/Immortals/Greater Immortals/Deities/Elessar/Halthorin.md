The Weaver of Fates was an elessar and a creation of [[Telos]] and [[Nehusta]]. He was married to [[Inarae]], the dreamer of stars. The two led the Elessar during [[Dur-baagal]]'s invasion of the [[Everforge]], and they trapped themselves within to seal it off.
