Malach was a vampire and dark wizard who had some amount of political power within a country that bordered Avmorei, and commanded a military attempt to attack various points at the border and intended to conquer Avmorei.[^1] 

[[Anremithrl Kuildel|Anremithrl]] and [[Lesalog Kuildel]] encountered one of these strike groups and were able to bring warning to the council.[^1]
