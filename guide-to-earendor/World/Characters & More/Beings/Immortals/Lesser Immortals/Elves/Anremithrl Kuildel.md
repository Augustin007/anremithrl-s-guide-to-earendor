Anremithrl was the twin brother of [[Lesalog Kuildel]]. They knew and trained for at least some time under [[Aulien Mithron]] and were soldiers in Arithmorei. 

Anremithrl was born mute, but taught himself to read and write at a very young age. He tends to be very closed and uncharismatic. He is keenly perceptive and telepathically linked to his brother. He is a drab, unable to manipulate magic at all. 

At one point he was a lookout at an outpost of the Dol Ibrith beacons.

He is tall and large, even among elves, silver-haired, and has skin so pale as to be white. He used to be armoured in black steel armour[^1], but eventually purchased one of Nullmetal from Theriad[^2]. 

He attempted to have his voice healed, but it was too large a part of his self-image and he could not get it healed.

There is no colour to be seen anywhere on his person. He has a scar across his face from when he saved his brother from a pack of wolves.

At some point he and his brother joined the Cobalt Archive, and he headed the Earendor History Project, though by the time of Naras, Anremithrl was long gone.
