The Alantothi family, often translated as "the mountains", was a family known for being even larger than other Goliaths, and for being stubborn, and for little else, as it was not one of the major clans. Felgar was a third child from somebody who was not of the ruling line of their clan, and thus was never expected to lead in anything.

Felgar was known to be exceptionally stubborn, even among his family, and did not give in easily.

Felgar worked in the quarry in his youth, being naturally larger and stronger than most, but when the eighteenth batallion passed through, he signed up to join them. 

His stubbornness sometimes caused some difficulties, but it was counteracted with his tenacity and determination to do well. 

He eventually was transferred to a cavalry division, where he met and loosely befriended Sjforn Thunukalathi. He was initially scared of horses. 

He would eventually be transferred again and promoted to a captain.

In the Siege of Thruk, he was left behind along with a small group under the command of Commander Felgar Steingeboren, to hold the pass as long as possible while a more coordinated regroup and retreat occurred. Commander Steingeboren killed himself, and many deserted, but Alantothi stepped up and took command. He led twenty men and held the pass for two months, running out of rations and nearly dying of starvation before reinforcements arrived in the form of the Siegebreakers. Often called the Avalanch Division. An elite cavalry led by the the Knightbreaker, Galio Thunukalathi, that specialised in reinforcing besieged locations and attacking besieging armies. The Siegebreakers broke the siege and drove off the enemies. The Knightbreaker offered Alantothi a position among the siegebreakers. 

As he recovered, he was promoted, given medals, and transferred to non-combative duty and took to bureaucracy extremely well, with the same stubbornness and tenacity, impressing many people. 

When he recovered, he put in a request to join the Siegebreakers, and Galio approved it. Alantothi quickly impressed Galio and rose through the ranks, and within a three years, had advanced to being Galio's second-in-command.

He would continue being Galio's second when Galio was promoted to High General of the unified NAF allied forces. 

Galio once beat him at arm-wrestling.

Alantothi took over for Galio when Galio was gravely injured by Stygian weaponry. During this time he and William Treaty did not get along very well. Felgar was sent in Galio's and travelled alongside Colossus and William to the NAF meeting, intending to update everyone on the situation and request more supplies and reinforcement, because Galio felt he couldn't go and that he had to remain coordinating the defense at Aldarkeep pass. When Asmodeus arrived at the NAF meeting, Alantothi concurred with Colossus, William, Singollo, Niamh, and Klaethor that Asmodeus could not be trusted. He did not leave with Colossus, William, and Singollo, but stayed behind with Klaethor and Niamh. The last time he was seen in campaign was through a hurried illusion-message, where Alantothi was wounded and spoke with Galio and delivered the news that Asmodeus had obtained leadership of the NAF and had had already Klaethor assasinated for speaking against him and had sent assassins after him, before succumbing to his wounds. Later communication with Niamh before her own assasination had her say that Alantothi gave his life so that she could escape with something, but Niamh was assasinated before reaching the rendez-vous point, and it has not yet been discovered what this was.
