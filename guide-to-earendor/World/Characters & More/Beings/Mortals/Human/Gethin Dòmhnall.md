Gethin was the seventh son of Lord Cormac Dòmhnall. He would go on to join the Kingsguard.

He would stand with the Moonwatchers against his own family many years later during the aftermath of the Fern Uprisings.

The captain of the Moonwatcher Kingsguard turned steward to [[Niamh Moonwatcher]], and raised [[Lancel Black-Moonwatcher]]
