DragonArrow was a company planned by [[William Treaty]] and [[Nil'ge'tar Remshi'ul|Nugget]]. Nugget died before it could be founded. The name Dragon refers to Nugget and Arrow refers to William.

William would found it later, and it would rise to great prominance. Leading up to the longest night, William managed to monopolise the entire shipping market.

The Chief Financial Officer was [[Baron Daron]].

The [[Analyst]] was an intern at DragonArrow.
