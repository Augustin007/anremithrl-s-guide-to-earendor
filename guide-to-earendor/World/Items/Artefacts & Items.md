The Frostbound Crown: Dur-baagal's crown, taken by Auril after her victory over him. She left a chunk of her divinity of it and left it behind in a vault of the nine hells when she returned to the material plane.

The Voidmirror. It was a dark, polished obsidian mirror, projecting the desires of individuals, its surface is a gateway into the dark regions, acting as a source of nearly unlimited negative quiddity, but siphoning their free will. It was shattered during the Sundering War.
