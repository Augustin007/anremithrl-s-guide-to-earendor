The Clockwork Bastion
A great plane formed into a single machine beyond mortal comprehension. The entire plane shifts and rearranges itself according to a preordained sequence known only to its creator. It houses the Engine Prime, a machine capable of analyzing and restructuring the topology of reality in its vicinity.

The Veil of Everchange
This realm is a kaleidoscope of shifting landscapes. Mountains dissolve into seas, and forests transform into fields of glass under skies that pulse with ever-changing hues. It is home to the Amaranthine Orb.

The Harmonium Nexus
The Harmonium Nexus is a sprawling city suspended in a web of energy. In it live many of the Elessar, for that is where they lived before the Eternal Wars, and from it much of Arvandor was designed. The Axis Key resides here, an artifact that allows its bearer to open pathways to any of the Architect’s Pockets
