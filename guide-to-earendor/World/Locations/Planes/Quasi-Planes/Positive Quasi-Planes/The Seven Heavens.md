#Planes_and_Regions/Lesser/Quasi-Planes/Material

A quasi-plane formmed between [[Elysium]] and the [[Material Plane]].

1. Lunia: The Silver Heaven, a realm of peace and tranquillity, bathed in eternal moonlight.
2. Mercuria: The Golden Heaven, a place of valour and courage, where the bravest souls reside.
3. Venya: The Pearly Heaven, a realm of endless beauty and artistry.
4. Solania: The Crystal Heaven, a place of wisdom and enlightenment.
5. Mertion: The Platinum Heaven, a realm of justice and law.
6. Jovar: The Glittering Heaven, a place of joy and happiness.
7. Chronias: The Illuminated Heaven, the highest plane, a realm of pure thought and energy, closest to the concept of ultimate good.
