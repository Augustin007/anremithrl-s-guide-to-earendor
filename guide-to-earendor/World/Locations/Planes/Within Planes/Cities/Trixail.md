Trixail was a city-state located near Neros and Karr that belonged to the wealthy ruling family. 

In 878 AH there was the Battle of the Aetate Foedus, a war between the Karr, Admaldari, Kuildel, Trixail, and Neros. It was fought near what would become Rymfel and lasted for two weeks, resulting in many casualties on every side.

In the year 1377 AH Trixail became one of the allied countries of the Aetate Pacis, along with Thilcerth, Iriondallyn, Birthlan, Elmuin, Paralamenos, Ardwedil, Kuildel, and Neros.

In the year 5395 AA, the Lord Moonwatcher Trixail was killed by Queen Tahlia Tormund of Rymfel in her invasion of the city.

Lady Niamh was the daughter of Moonwatcher inherited her father's position and became the lady of Trixail. She was well-rounded in her education and was trained in various skills such as music, dancing, and swordsmanship. However, she faced many challenges during her reign, including political conflicts with Rymfel and dealing with the Greenclad thieves' guild.
