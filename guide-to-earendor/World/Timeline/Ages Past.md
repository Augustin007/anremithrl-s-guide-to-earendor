

---

**The Premortal Ages**

First, there was [[Beyond time]], the Almost. That which Caused time to form and flow. 

Thereafter came [[The Age of the Primordials]], the great beings who shaped the planes and forces.

But they were not at piece, and thus began the [[The Eternal Wars]].

And that concluded and from there began [[The Age of The Gods]].

---

**The Ancient Ages**

And the gods created mortals, and thus began [[The Age of Awakening]].

And [[The Age of Building]], as far back as civilisations can be traced.

And then [[The Age of Rising]], when mortals grew in prominence and knowledge and spoke often with the gods.

[[The Sundering War]], when civil war between gods ripped apart the worlds.

---

**The Ages of Sanctuary**

And after the war, the Ley Lines and were established through the Tree of Sanctuary, and there was peace, and [[The Age of Wandering]] was called, as mortals rebuilt.

[[The Age of Shadows]], when darkness attempted to break through the sanctuary.

Thereafter, with kingdoms and empires rebuilt, [[The Age of Heroes]] threw off the darkness.

And thus began [[The Age of Harmony]].

---

**The Modern Ages**

And when the Tree of Sanctuary was destroyed, thus began [[The Age of Ash]] (current age).
