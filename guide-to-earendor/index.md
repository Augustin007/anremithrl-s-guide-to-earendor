# Anremithrl’s Guide to Earendor

I am [[Anremithrl Kuildel]], of the [[Cobalt Archive]]. It is my duty to compile and comprehend the histories of the world.

---

This archive does not present a single “true” version of events.

Instead, it reflects how knowledge is actually preserved in Eärendor:

- Multiple calendars coexist.
    
- Dates are approximate when records disagree.
    
- Divine testimony is treated as biased.
    
- Mortal historians are fallible.
    
- Some facts are deliberately suppressed, lost, or misattributed.
    

Primary sources are quoted when available. Later commentary is clearly distinguished from original accounts. Where legends harden into doctrine, that process is documented rather than corrected.
